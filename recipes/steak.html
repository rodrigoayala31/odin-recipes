Steak

Description

A perfectly cooked steak is a savory, juicy cut of beef, seared to form a caramelized crust on the outside while remaining tender and flavorful inside.
Rich in aroma and bursting with bold, meaty taste, it often features beautiful grill marks and a pink, succulent center that melts in your mouth.
Whether served with a side of vegetables or paired with a rich sauce, steak is a timeless favorite that satisfies with every bite.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2-3 springs of rosemary

Steps

  1. Bring steak to room temperature
  2. Season generously
  3. Heat the pan
  4. Sear the steak
  5. Add butter and aromatics
  6. Check doneness
  7. Rest the steak
