Caramel Flan

Description

Caramel flan is a smooth and creamy custard dessert topped with a rich layer of golden caramel. Silky in texture and subtly sweet, this classic treat melts in your mouth with each spoonful.
Made with simple ingredients like eggs, milk, and sugar, flan is baked gently in a water bath to achieve its delicate consistency and glossy finish.
Perfectly chilled and inverted to reveal its glistening caramel top, it's a timeless dessert loved across many cultures.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) water
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 can (14 oz / 397g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz / 354ml) evaporated milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Make the caramel:
    1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the sugar and water.
    2. Cook without stirring until the sugar melts and turns a deep golden brown.
    3. Immediately pour the caramel into a round cake pan or individual ramekins, swirling to coat the bottom evenly. Set aside to cool and harden.
  2. Prepare the custard:
    1. In a mixing bowl, whisk the eggs.
    2. Add the sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt (optional). Mix until smooth.
    3. Pour the custard mixture over the hardened caramel in the pan.
  3. Bake the flan:
    1. Place the pan in a larger baking dish and fill the outer dish with hot water (a water bath) until it reaches halfway up the sides of the flan pan.
    2. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for about 50–60 minutes, or until the center is set but still slightly jiggly.
  4. Cool and Chill
  5. Unmold and Serve
